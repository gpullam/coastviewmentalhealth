About Us

Coastline Mental Health is a behavioral health clinic located in Niceville, Florida. We take a holistic, evidence-based approach to treating our patients while maintaining the highest standards of care.

We understand how stressful it can be to find a healthcare provider, so we make it easy with an online appointment system found here.

Treatments

We offer medication management and psychotherapy for many conditions at our clinic, including but not limited to:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Attention Deficit Disorders
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Schizophrenia
  • Bipolar Disorder

Our Qualifications

Brian Pullam is the owner of our practice. Certified as both a Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and a Family Nurse Practitioner, Brian has a unique perspective on how to achieve positive outcomes with our patients.

Brian has over 13 years of nursing experience (med-surg, oncology, and hospice), earning his Master’s Degree from Frontier Nursing University in 2019, and a post-master certification in Psychiatric Mental Health in 2023.

Brian’s wide experience with many aspects of healthcare uniquely qualify him to provide quality care to patients from children to the elderly, providing a holistic approach and taking into account physical health as well as mental health.

Brian was born and raised in Niceville, giving him a deep knowledge of the needs of the local community.
